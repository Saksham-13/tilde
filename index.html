What is Tilde

Tilde is an initiative by HackerSpace PESUECC to encourage students to build projects and pick up real-world, industry-standard skills on the way.

What does someone in Tilde do?

Build something awesome! Pick a project to work on, and get started by making your first pull request

Who can join?

How does it work?

1. We organize students in to small, five-person teams which focus on building one project.
2. Every single project will be Open-Source, and built in a public Github Repository.
3. Students work on the project in small, two-week cycles. Every two weeks, you get to decide whether or not to continue with the project.

How do I join an existing project?

Projects under Tilde are always looking for developers who are passionate and wanna make their significant open source contribution. Maintaining an open source community needs it own few set of rules, joining any of these projects under tilde assumes that you will abide by these rules.
- See a interesting project that and has an opening [Note : All requirements are listed in the projects page which can be opened by Hovering over the project and clicking "open"
- Contact our fellow admins at HackerSpaceECC, DO NOT CONTACT THE MAINTAINER of the project [Note : This breaks due process].
- On being accepted, you will receive a mail, the same will also be indicated as being part of team in `Tilde Projects` page.
